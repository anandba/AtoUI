Recruitment Services

Mission

Problem

As Japan is going through an era of decrease in working population, the government is pushing forward for companies to hire Global Talent. However, there are many issues remaining such as low hiring rate of exchange students, law violation in the Technical Intern Training Program, and low adaptation rate of high skilled workers.

What We Do ?

Erai as a team with Japanese-cultural backgrounds will support the "Global Talent that want to build their career in Japan" and "Companies that need the power of Global Talent" through our business pillars based on Education - Recruitment - Adaptation to ultimately become an "Infrastructure that connects Global Talent with Japan"

How We Do ?

IT specific training of Japanese language: 6 months full time intensive training with project drills under the close guidance of a Japanese technical manager with more emphasis on Japanese business and social culture.
Adapt Horenso, japanese culture training,“Hear one, understand ten” (ichi ieba ju wo shiru)
