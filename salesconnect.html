Smart ERP Connect is Business management software that allows an organization to use a system of integrated applications to manage the business.

Why Smart ERP Connect?

Smart ERP Connect is a versatile, widely-implemented solution that many companies use to facilitate business processes. Because of the range of customization and configuration options available, you can tailor solutions to your specific needs and to the existing software that you already use. By automating sometimes challenging and often tedious workflows, Smart ERP Connect aim to make your day-to-day workflow a little easier.

How Smart ERP Connect Works ?:

  • At its most basic level, Smart ERP Connect integrates these various functions into one complete system to streamline processes and information across the entire organization. The central feature of Smart ERP Connect is a shared database that supports multiple functions used by different business units.

The Key Benefits that Smart ERP Connect offers are:

  • Cost Effective for Small Scale Enterprises, Start Ups
  • Customization as per needs
  • A Common Database that supports all the modules
  • A Consistent Customizable Look and Feel across modules
  • Increase in productivity with less Human Interventions
  • Master Data Management

Features of Smart ERP Connect

  • Cloud Based : No software maintainace
  • Fully Responsive Web system : Can be used on Mobiles, Tablets, Laptops
  • AI enabled to reduce Human Efforts
  • Backup and Access Anywhere, Clean Interface
  • Secure, Easy to use.

Application areas of Smart ERP Connect

  • Financial Management
  • Sales and Customer Management
  • Project Management
  • Product and Service Management
  • Employee Management
  • HR Management
