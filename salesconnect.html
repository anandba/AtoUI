Smart ERP Connect




Smart ERP Connect is Business management software that allows an organization to use a system of integrated applications to manage the business.

How It Works?

  • Smart ERP Connect is a versatile, widely-implemented solution that many companies use to facilitate business processes.
  • Because of the range of customization and configuration options available, you can tailor solutions to your specific needs and to the existing software that you already use.
  • By automating sometimes challenging and often tedious workflows, Smart ERP Connect aim to make your day-to-day workflow a little easier.
  • At its most basic level, Smart ERP Connect integrates these various functions into one complete system to streamline processes and information across the entire organization.
  • The central feature of Smart ERP Connect is a shared database that supports multiple functions used by different business units.

Key Benefits

  • Cost Effective for Small Scale Enterprises, Start Ups
  • Customization OF UI/Features as per need
  • A Common Database that supports all the modules
  • A Consistent Customizable Look and Feel across modules
  • Increase in productivity with less Human Interventions
  • Master Data Management
